Empowering acoustic research at scale

Empowering ecologists and researchers to create stunning portfolios of animal vocalizations. Our platform makes it easy to listen, analyze, and share the acoustic signatures of wildlife. From field recordings to curated libraries and peer collaboration, Call Detective helps teams turn noisy datasets into publication-grade insights.

Built for both solo researchers and large teams, the platform supports automated tagging, batch processing, and reproducible analysis pipelines. Use integrated visualization tools to explore spectrograms, annotate calls, and export curated datasets for publications or public repositories.

  • mic High-fidelity recording — Record and upload lossless files and maintain original metadata.
  • analytics AI-assisted identification — Quickly detect and classify species calls with confidence scores.
  • folder_shared Collaborative workflows — Share projects, annotate together, and manage contributor roles.
  • public Open science tools — Export reproducible analysis and publish datasets alongside research.
