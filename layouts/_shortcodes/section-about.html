About Call Detective

Call Detective is a cutting-edge platform designed specifically for ecologists, wildlife researchers, and conservation biologists who work with acoustic data. Our mission is to make bioacoustic research more accessible, collaborative, and impactful.

Our Mission

We believe that understanding animal communication is key to protecting biodiversity. By providing tools to record, analyze, and share wildlife sounds, we help researchers build comprehensive acoustic libraries that contribute to conservation efforts worldwide.
