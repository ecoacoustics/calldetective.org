Share acoustic soundscapes

Call detective websites are fully customizable platforms. Allowing you to share any media such as youtube promotional videos, images and audio clips to highlight the unique soundscapes of your study area.

You can also provide context about your research project, the study area, and the species of interest to engage your audience and encourage participation.

Collaborate with citizen scientists

Data platforms such as Ecosounds are powerful tools that include data ingestion, analysis, and annotating workflows. However, interacting with citizen scientists through a data platform is often daunting and a barrier to engagement.

Call detective websites are designed to complement data platforms by providing an engaging and user-friendly interface for citizen scientists to explore and contribute to acoustic data.
