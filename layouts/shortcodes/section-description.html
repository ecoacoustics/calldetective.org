Call detective websites are designed to be simple, engaging websites, allowing you to share promotional videos, images, and audio clips to show off your project.

The Whoo's Calling project has used rich content to showcase the range of owls. Providing clear examples of male, female, and chick calls to help citizen scientists identify different species.

Collaborate with citizen scientists

Data platforms such as Ecosounds are powerful tools that include data ingestion, analysis, and annotating workflows. However, interacting with citizen scientists through a data management platform is a barrier to engagement.

Call detective websites are designed to complement data platforms by providing an engaging and user-friendly interface for citizen scientists to explore and contribute to ecoacoustics projects.
